Street Fighter 1987

Street Fighter (comumente chamado de Street Fighter I) é um jogo de arcade lançado no ano de 1987 e desenvolvido pela Capcom. Foi o primeiro jogo de luta feito pela empresa e também o primeiro jogo da série Street Fighter. Embora não tenha alcançado a mesma popularidade que suas sequências quando foi lançado pela primeira vez, o Street Fighter original introduziu algumas mecânica que se tornaram padrão em jogos posteriores, como botões de ataque e técnicas especiais baseadas em comandos. Uma porta para o TurboGrafx-CD foi lançado com o título Fighting Street em 1988.

Data de Lançamento: 30 de Agosto de 1987

Platformas: Arcade, TurboGrafx-CD, PC, Amstrad CPC, ZX Spectrum, Commodore 64, Amiga, Virtual Console, Xbox, PlayStation 2, PlayStation Portable, Nintendo Switch

Desenvolvedora: Capcom

Distribuidora: Capcom

Designer: Hiroshi Matsumoto

PERSONAGENS:

Nome País Aparência Sobre
Ryu Japão A Japanese martial artist who enters the Street Fighter tournament to prove his strength. He can use the Hadoken, Shoryuken, and the Tatsumaki Senpukyaku.
Ken Masters Estado Unidos Ken does not officially participate in the Street Fighter tournament. Instead, he departs to fight in and eventually win the US Martial Arts tournament. Due to having the same Sensei as Ryu, he uses the same three moves as his friend and rival.
Retsu Japão Retsu is a Shorinji Kempo instructor who was excommunicated for using forbidden moves while getting into street fights.
Geki Japão Geki is an anonymous ninja who fights with a talon, shurikens and ninjutsu illusions.
Joe Estado Unidos Joe was formerly an underground martial art champion before his time as an outlaw forced him on the run.
Mike Estado Unidos Mike is a former heavyweight pugilist who once killed an opponent in the ring and did time in jail for robbery, where he learned the art of boxing.
Lee China Lee is a mysterious Bajiquan expert who enters the Street Fighter tournament to put Chinese Kung-Fu back on the mainstream.
Gen China Gen is an elderly professional assassin who has developed his own murderous Kung-Fu style and enters the Street Fighter tournament to find worthy challengers.
Birdie Inglaterra Birdie is a giant bouncer who uses a combination of wrestling and boxing techniques, this punker is dangerous when jumping in mid-air.
Eagle Inglaterra Eagle is a boujutsu expert who is the hired bodyguard of a wealthy family who is rivals with Sagat but was previously defeated by the latter. His weapon of choice is fighting with two Escrima/Kali sticks.
Adon Tailândia Adon is the number one disciple of Sagat. He is participating in the Street Fighter tournament to prove to the world that he is more than just a lesser version of the "Emperor of Muay Thai".
Sagat Tailândia Sagat is the "Emperor of Muay Thai”, the greatest Muay Thai fighter there is. But that isn't enough for him. He wants to be the greatest fighter in the world! He hosted the Street Fighter tournament to prove it, and great warriors from around the world also came to prove their strength. He fires Tiger Shots and also uses the hopping Tiger Knee.
